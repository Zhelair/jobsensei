# JobSensei Shorts Script Bank

Practical short-form video concepts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Use Notes

- Recommended format: `9:16`
- Recommended duration: `12-18s`
- Caption style: large burned-in captions, `3-6` words per line
- Editing rhythm: cut every `1.5-3s`
- Positioning: lead with pain or humor, then reveal JobSensei as the workflow fix
- Sound strategy: pick a current sound in the same vibe on posting day

## Reusable Visual Motifs

## Reusable Phrases

- `Stop applying blind.`
- `Paste the JD first.`
- `Know your gaps before the recruiter does.`
- `Practice before the panic.`
- `From rejection spiral to prep plan.`
- `Your interview prep should not start 20 minutes before the call.`

## Posting Formula

- `70%` pain or humor
- `20%` app reveal
- `10%` CTA

## Suggested Workflow

1. Record raw screen footage in OBS or directly on your phone.
2. Cut the clips in CapCut, Shotcut, or DaVinci Resolve.
3. Add captions first, then sound.
4. Replace the sound with a fresh sound in the same vibe on posting day.
5. Post the same base video to TikTok, Reels, and Shorts with slightly different captions.
