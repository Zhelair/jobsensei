What this extension does

It saves the job page you are viewing into JobSensei so you can research, prepare, and follow up from one workspace.

1

Reads the current job page.
It tries to capture the company, role, URL, and visible job description.

2

Lets you review before sending.
You can fix messy page data before it enters JobSensei.

3

Creates an application workspace.
JobSensei opens the role with capture, research, notes, and prep tools ready.

Does it apply to jobs for me?

No. It only captures information into JobSensei. You stay in control of applications and submissions.

What data is sent?

Only the fields shown in the Capture tab: company, role, job URL, and job description text.

What if capture is wrong?

Edit the fields first, or press Refresh Capture after scrolling/opening the job details.
