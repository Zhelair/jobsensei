What this extension does

It turns a job page into a JobSensei application workspace for research, notes, and prep.

1

Captures the current job page.
Company, role, URL, and visible JD text are pulled into one review screen.

2

Captures selected JD text too.
Highlight the JD, right-click, and choose the JobSensei capture action.

3

Opens the right workspace.
JobSensei updates an existing matching application or creates a new one.

Does it apply to jobs for me?

No. It only captures information into JobSensei. You stay in control of applications and submissions.

What data is sent?

Only the fields shown in the Capture tab: company, role, job URL, and job description text.

What if capture is wrong?

Edit the fields first, use selected-text capture for the JD, or press Refresh Capture after opening the job details.
