JobSensei Pricing

Simple plans for job-search prep with hosted AI or your own key.

JobSensei offers a Free plan for light hosted usage and a Pro plan for deeper ongoing prep. New purchases and renewals are processed through Paddle on approved JobSensei domains. Legacy Buy Me a Coffee access is still honored for existing supporters.

Start with secure email sign-in and a monthly hosted-AI allowance.

  • 531 hosted AI credits every 31 days
  • Current hosted request cost: 31 credits per request

  • Up to 2 approved devices on the same account
  • BYOK mode still available if you want to use your own provider key

Built for active interview prep, repeat applications, and heavier hosted usage.

  • 53,000 hosted AI credits every 31 days
  • Current hosted request cost: 31 credits per request
  • Roughly 1,709 hosted requests each month
  • Secure account linking with the same purchase email
  • Manage approved devices and app access inside JobSensei
  • Manage payments, invoices, renewals, and cancellations through Paddle

Billing notes

Prices shown on this page are the standard public JobSensei plans. Pro is billed at EUR 5 per month. Taxes may be added by Paddle at checkout where required.

Free and Pro allowances are currently based on the live credit model inside the app. If the hosted request cost or included monthly credits change later, this page will be updated.
