Refund Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Pricing Terms of Service Privacy Policy

JobSensei is a digital software product. This policy explains when refunds may be approved for Pro purchases processed through Paddle.

First-time Pro purchases

A first-time Pro purchase may be eligible for a refund if you request it within 14 days of the initial charge and the service has not been materially used after purchase.

Renewals

Renewal charges may be reviewed for refund when the request is made within 72 hours of the renewal and the renewed period has not been materially used.

When refunds are usually not granted

  • When a large portion of the included hosted AI usage has already been consumed.
  • When the request is made long after the charge date.
  • When access was suspended for abuse, fraud, or terms violations.
  • For partial-month requests after a billing period has already been substantially used.

How to cancel

Cancel future renewals through Paddle using the link on your receipt, purchase email, or the Manage Billing button inside JobSensei when it is available on your account. Canceling stops future renewals. It does not automatically refund the current billing period, and access usually remains active until the current paid period ends.

How to request a refund

Use the support contact listed on your Paddle receipt or purchase email. Include the purchase email address, charge date, and a short reason for the request so it can be reviewed quickly.

Chargebacks and duplicate charges

If you were charged more than once for the same purchase or believe a payment was made in error, request help first so the issue can be reviewed and resolved directly as quickly as possible.
